Our Story

A Culinary Tribute to Royal Bikaner

"Each crunchy bite immediately takes you to the vibrant and historic climes of this royal city."

Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad is a culinary tribute to the rich heritage of Bikaner, Rajasthan. The familiarity of this adored snack brings back cherished moments of being fed Papads alongside Dal-Chawal by our mothers.

The art of Papad-making has been perfected for generations in Bikaner. Crafted with the finest ingredients and inspired by the years-old cooking secrets of our grandmothers, Haldiram's replicates traditional methods to create this Namkeen snack — keeping it close to time-honoured recipes.

Common Questions

FAQ

What does 'Katran' signify in Bikaneri Katran Papad? +
The term 'Katran' in Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad signifies its distinctive appearance — thin, shredded pieces of Papad. Unlike traditional round Papads, Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad is cut into small pieces, making it perfect for snacking.
Can I reheat Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad after it becomes soft? +
Yes, you can reheat Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad in a dry skillet or frying pan until it becomes crispy again. Just a minute or two on medium heat restores the satisfying crunch.
What is an innovative way to serve Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad? +
If you want to serve Haldiram's Bikaneri Katran Papad in an innovative way, mix it with well-chopped tomatoes and onions, add a pinch of salt and Chaat Masala. Add peanuts and a hint of chilli flakes to level up your spice game!
How should I store Bikaneri Katran Papad to keep it fresh? +
Store the Papad in a cool, dry place away from moisture and direct sunlight. Once opened, seal the packet tightly or transfer to an airtight container to maintain freshness and crunch.

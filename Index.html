FAQ

Bikaneri Katran Papad mein 'Katran' ka kya matlab hai?+
'Katran' ka matlab hai papad ke patle, chhote aur shredded pieces. Round papad se alag, yeh snack format mein perfect rehta hai.
Agar papad soft ho jaye to kya use dubara crisp kiya ja sakta hai?+
Haan, dry pan ya skillet par medium heat mein 1-2 minute reheat karke papad ko dubara crispy banaya ja sakta hai.
Khichda Papad ko thoda hatke style mein kaise serve karein?+
Tomato, onion, thoda namak, chaat masala, peanuts aur chilli flakes ke saath mix karke aap ek instant chatpata bowl bana sakte hain.
Bikaneri Katran Papad ko fresh rakhne ke liye kaise store karein?+
Papad ko cool aur dry jagah par rakhein. Packet khulne ke baad airtight container mein store karein.
Kya yeh product PAN India delivery mein available hai?+
Haan, yeh product PAN India deliver hota hai. Standard delivery time 3-5 working days hai.
